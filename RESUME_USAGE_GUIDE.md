## ðŸ’¡ Resume Customization Tips

### For Each Job Application:

1. **Read the Job Description Carefully**
   - Highlight required skills
   - Note preferred qualifications
   - Identify keywords

2. **Customize Your Resume (5 minutes)**
   - Add their keywords to your summary
   - Reorder skills to match their priorities
   - Highlight relevant projects
   - Adjust job descriptions to emphasize matching experience

3. **Example: If They Want "Microservices Experience"**
   
   **Generic:**
   > "Built Node.js backend with RESTful API"
   
   **Customized:**
   > "Architected microservices-based Node.js backend with RESTful API supporting 10K+ requests/month"

4. **Match Their Language**
   - If they say "React" and you know Vue, mention "modern JavaScript frameworks"
   - If they say "AWS" and you use Firebase, mention "cloud platforms (Firebase, AWS)"
   - If they say "Agile," mention your SCRUM experience
